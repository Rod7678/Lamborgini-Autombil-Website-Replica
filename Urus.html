Lamborghini Urus is the world’s first Super Sport Utility Vehicle, in which luxury, sportiness and performance meet comfort and versatility. It offers best-in-class driving dynamics, alongside its unmistakable elegance of design. Urus embodies the characteristics of multiple souls: sporty, elegant and off-road, and has a suitability for everyday driving in a range of environments. With its surprisingly distinct engine sound, combined with high performance, Lamborghini Urus is anything but typical.

OVERVIEW

The soul of a super sports car and the functionality of an SUV: Lamborghini Urus is the first Super Sport Utility Vehicle in the world. With extreme proportions, breathtaking design, extraordinary driving dynamics and heart-pounding performance, Urus represents freedom in its quintessential state. You can experience any road, from track to the sand, ice, gravel or rocks, thus unlocking any road. You can explore any new terrain, thus expressing yourself.

INTERIOR

Revolutionary thinking is at the heart of every idea from Automobili Lamborghini. Whether it is aerospace-inspired design or technologies applied to the naturally aspirated V12 engine or carbon-fiber structure, going beyond accepted limits is part of our philosophy. The Aventador advances every concept of performance, immediately establishing itself as the benchmark for the super sports car sector. Giving a glimpse of the future today, it comes from a family of supercars already considered legendary.
